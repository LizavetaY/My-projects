How to play

How to fill the main page:

  • First input: It affords you to choose Level of difficulty. You can choose the number of cards in the row and column displayed thanks to the inserted value into the input.

    * Be careful: this value is not a quantity of cards pairs or of all cards in the game

    There are available values:
    • 2 cards - 2 pars
    • 4 cards - 8 pars
    • 6 cards - 18 pars
    • 8 cards - 32 pars
    • 10 cards - 50 pars
  • Second checkbox: If you check it, the game will go with the timer. The timer value depends on cards you insert into the first input:
    • 2 cards - 5 sec
    • 4 cards - 25 sec
    • 6 cards - 50 sec
    • 8 cards - 85 sec
    • 10 cards - 130 sec
  • Start game button: It will start the game with your inserted data (the timer can be turned off)

How to play in the game pairs:

  • Open cards in the field and find same
  • Only two card can be opened in one time
  • Found pairs will be opened and deactivated
  • You can finish the game at any time
  • If you play with the timer, hurry up to find all pairs in time
  • You can pause the game, if you need a break during playing the game with the timer
  • Don't be afraid to lose your game during reloading. It will be saved
  • And you can load a new game with similar conditions, when the main game is finished
